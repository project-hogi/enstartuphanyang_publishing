Venturing Space Sponsored by Alumni

Venturing Space Sponsored by Alumni is a business incubating space in which alumni venture entrepreneurs directly support the fees for using startup spaces by beginning entrepreneurs, mentoring, investment connection, and follow-up management.

  • Location
    ㆍB2, Hanyang Institute of Technology (HIT), Hanyang University, 222 Wangshimni-ro, Seongdong-gu, Seoul
  • Eligibility
    ㆍProspective entrepreneurs and startup companies that have been 3 years or less since the foundation
  • Support Details
    ㆍProvides a free space
    ㆍMentoring support of specialized fields such as legal affairs, taxation, labor, patent, etc.
    ㆍConnection with the opportunities of startup funds investment through Hanyang Angel Fund and Hanyang Youth Startup Fund
    ㆍPreference on participation in startup education and networking programs
  • Selection Process
    ㆍ1st: Document screening -> 2nd: Presentation evaluation -> Final selection
