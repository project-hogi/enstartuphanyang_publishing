Global Startup Internships

In this internship program, students can experience a series of global startup process such as market research and corporate foundation by performing the projects of overseas and domestic companies.

  • Period
    ㆍ5 weeks during summer/winter breaks
  • Place
    ㆍ(U.S.) Silicon Valley · New York · Las Vegas, (China) Shanghai · Beijing, (Indonesia) Jakarta · Bali, (Vietnam) Hanoi · Ho Chi Minh, (Canada) Ottawa, etc.
  • Eligible Participants
    ㆍHanyang University students
  • Administrator
    ㆍHanyang University Center for Global Entrepreneurship

Domestic Startup Internships

This internship program allows students to gain on-site industry experiences and solidify startup foundation through this in the promising startups of Business Incubator Center.

  • Period
    ㆍ7~8 weeks during summer/winter breaks
  • Place
    ㆍCompanies that moved into the on-campus business incubator center
  • Eligible Participants
    ㆍHanyang University students
  • Administrator
    ㆍHanyang University Center for Global Entrepreneurship
