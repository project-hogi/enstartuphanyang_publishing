Startup Club

Startup Club discovers and supports startup clubs that have fresh and original ideas, provide excellent club startup spaces, etc., and support production.

  • Purpose
    ㆍDiscover and support college student startup clubs that have fresh and new ideas, create a positive atmosphere for startups, and promote youth startups
  • Eligibility
    ㆍTeams with more than 3 members (including the representative of startup club) consisting of Hanyang University students and students on leave of absence, who have fresh and original ideas and have startup determination
    ㆍThe representative must be a Hanyang University student (including students on leave of absence) (can recruit other university students as additional members)
  • Selection Process
    ㆍSubmit startup club application forms -> Document screening -> Interview evaluation
  • Support Details
    ㆍProvide a common startup preparation space for the students of excellent startup clubs
    ㆍDifferentiated support of item development cost (prototype production support)
    ㆍSupport corporate foundation commission when establishing a company
    ㆍSupport expert mentoring and consulting
    ㆍSupport the acquisition of intellectual property right
    ㆍHelp connection with other startup support programs
