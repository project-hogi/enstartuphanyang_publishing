Hanyang Tech Venture Program

Hanyang Tech Venture Program continuously manages and sponsors students by building multilateral cooperation relationships and ecology with domestic startup-related institutions. By doing so, it seeks to produce a few startup entrepreneurs who have a tough spirit through entrepreneurship and by continuously checking their startup competence.

Benefits
Development of Hanyang Tech Venture Program (HTVP) which directly support the entire cycle of startup, from the development to the commercialization of a new business model that combines the 11 university technologies of next generation growth engine and unused ideas of large companies.

Expected Effects
ㆍContribute to stable business development by making intellectual property rights for startup items
ㆍCan create a new business model combining university-owned technology and the unused ideas of large companies
