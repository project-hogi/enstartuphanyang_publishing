Mentoring Cafe

In this mentoring program, alumni mentors pass on their experiences and wisdom to their mentees such as students and beginning entrepreneurs, and give advice on planning for life and career.

  • Date
    1 regular meeting a year, frequent flashmob meetings per mentor
  • Participants
    100 of prospective entrepreneurs (including startup clubs), entrepreneurs, etc.
  • Details
    4~5 CEO mentors, each mentor is assigned with 20~30 mentees, have mentoring hours

  • ㆍMentors share their rich experiences and wisdom with their mentee
  • ㆍEncourage mentees (beginning entrepreneurs) and instill challenge spirit and sense of responsibility
  • ㆍDeliver messages to junior entrepreneurs as the seniors of life

Mentor’s on Call

Mentor’s on Call is a 1:1 on/offline customized real-time mentoring program.

  • Eligible Participants
    Student entrepreneurs (prospective entrepreneurs), startup clubs, moved-in companies, students of startup lectures, start-up academy students, other alumni entrepreneurs, etc.
  • Organization of the Mentoring Team
    Organization of the Mentoring Team: divided into general, specialized, and peer mentoring teams
    - General: Intellectual property right, legal affairs, tax/accounting, finance, development, design, translation, human resources, etc.
    - Specialized: Business coaching including commercialization, marketing, attracting investment, overseas expansion
    - Peer: 1:1 and group mentoring for youth CEOs
